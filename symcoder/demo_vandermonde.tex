\section{About this document}
This document demonstrates the Phase-3 Vandermonde one-shot encoder running on its own, with Phase 1 and Phase 2 disabled.  The Vandermonde encoder is deterministically faithful on the full rep alignment table, so it is a valid standalone encoder; this document shows exactly what it consumes (the rep atoms and the alignment table $T$) and what it emits.\\[1ex]
\textbf{Mode}: \textbf{Mode:} \texttt{Cinf}\\\\
\textbf{Scale (lambda)}: \textbf{Scale ($\lambda$):} \texttt{4}\\\\
See \texttt{DOCS/phase3\_vandermonde.pdf} for the mathematical derivation (DFT-node power sums, conjugate-pair compression, optional dimensional rescaling).\\[1ex]
